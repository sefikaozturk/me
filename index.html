Intro

Hi! I'm Sefika.

I'm a Computer Science and Math student at Vanderbilt University, where I am involved with the Ingram Scholars Program, Center for Entrepreneurship, and previously, The Vanderbilt Hustler.

I love building things. I have built underwater robots that dive 200m into the sea, a multiagent research environment for the Polytopia game, a fermionic information theory library for Python, and more.

Currently, I'm a co-founder at Invariant building agentic infrastructure, as well as an intern at Good Start Labs doing AI evals research.

I am based in San Francisco for the summer.

Let's chat! Book a time or email me.
